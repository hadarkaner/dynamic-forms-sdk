# Demo Videos

Two short videos — the developer side (the portal) and the end-user side (filling out the form in the app) — together illustrate the core value: an edit in the portal shows up in the app with no rebuild.

## 1. Developer side — editing and publishing in the portal

<video controls width="100%" src="/videos/developer-side.mp4"></video>

Logging into the portal, editing an existing form, publishing a new version, and assigning/swapping a form through **Form Slots**.

## 2. User side — filling out the form in the app

<video controls class="phone-video" src="/videos/fill-form.mp4"></video>

The "order successful" screen in the app, the bottom sheet opening, filling in the rating and a free-text field, submitting, and the automatic transition to the "Thank you" screen.
